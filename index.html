Old Hagia Sophia

3D Reconstruction and Analysis of Hidden Structures

About

We are a small interdisciplinary student team from Furtwangen University (HFU), working within the framework of our project studies. In collaboration with the Byzantine Studies Department at the University of Freiburg, we are developing a digital application that enables the exploration and analysis of archaeological findings beneath the present-day Hagia Sophia.

Using advanced 3D reconstruction techniques, we digitally model these substructures to make them accessible for academic research and public education. Our aim is to bridge the gap between archaeology and technology by creating immersive, interactive tools that bring the hidden history of this iconic site to life.
